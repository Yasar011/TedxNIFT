About TEDxNIFTJodhpur

TEDxNIFTJodhpur provides a platform to educate and enlighten young minds on a variety of subjects through talks, motivating them to innovate, invent, and initiate conversations that matter. The 18-minute talks are idea-focused, wide-ranging, and thought-provoking.
