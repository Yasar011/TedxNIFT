About TEDxNIFTJodhpur

TEDxNIFTJodhpur provides a platform to educate and enlighten young minds on a variety of subjects through talks, motivating them to innovate, invent, and initiate conversations that matter. The 18-minute talks are idea-focused, wide-ranging, and thought-provoking.

Aarohan, meaning ascent or rising, captures the spirit of growth, transformation, and the pursuit of higher possibilities. As a TEDx theme, it symbolizes the journey of ideas, innovations, and individuals who challenge limits and rise above obstacles to create impact. It reflects the power of moving upward—whether through knowledge, creativity, resilience, or social change. TEDx Aarohan becomes a platform to celebrate stories of elevation: people who have climbed against odds, ideas that have uplifted communities, and innovations that continue to push humanity forward. Just like every ascent is marked by courage and vision, this theme invites speakers and audiences alike to rise, evolve, and shape the future together.
